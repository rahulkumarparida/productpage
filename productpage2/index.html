August Horch

August Horch is the founder of Audi. In 1899, he founded the Horch & Cie automotive group and built his first vehicle, which included six-cylinder and inline-four engine models. These models would later become the basis for Audi models. Ten years later, in 1909, Horch founded Audi Automobilwerke in Zwickau, but he couldn't use his own name for the company for competitive reasons. Instead, he translated his name, which means "hark" or "listen" in German, into Latin to create the name Audi
